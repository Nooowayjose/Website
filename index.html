Brief bio

Web developer, engineer, and explorer. Jose Cabrera finds joy in exploring the unknown and going on random treasure-hunting trips.

Jose recently earned a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Houston. He is a Michael and Susan Dell Foundation Scholar and first of his family to attend university. In addition, he completed an internship/contract position at NASA (through MORI Associates) this past summer.

He’s actively working on a wide array of side projects, from developing websites for adoptable dogs to augmented reality card games (and even breast cancer detection systems).

From time to time, Jose loves to casually go on treasure hunting trips with his dog, Toby. He’s collected over 35 geocaches to date and plans to collect at least 100 more this year.
